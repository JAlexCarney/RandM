Games

These are all the Game projects I have worked on or am currently working. Every project is available to be played for free without downloading anything. There is also a design journal associated with each project documenting progress and design methods.

Infinite Fire Zombies

Infinite Fire Zombies (working title) is My personal testing ground for the algorithms and techniques I learn In my University Courses. Things like A* search, Decision Trees, and Procedural Content Generation with Simplex Noise. It is heavily inspired by the game Advanced Wars for the Game Boy Advance.

Food Truck: The Game

Food Truck: The Game is a simulation game I worked on with Janel Catajoy and Amanda Leiserowitz. It was created in 5 weeks and was submitted as my final project for the junior level game development course at University of California Santa Cruz.

Sisyphus

This game was designed to explore the nature of mortality as well as practice my ability to use Unity to make games. This game was created in 2 weeks and was submitted as my final project for my freshman level game development course at University of California Santa Cruz.
